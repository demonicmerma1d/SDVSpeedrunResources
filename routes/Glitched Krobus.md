# Route
## Notes
- Version 1.6.
- Female gender.
- Chinese.

## Overview
The goal here is to roommate Krobus, primarily utilising mail injection and some item injection to inject friendship. 

### File Creation
Name the player the formatted linked paste and do not skip the introduction.

### Introduction
Skip after Robin says the player name. 

### Day 1
- Read the mail and move the bed to the door, then run down to the sewer to talk to Krobus.
- Exhaust yourself on the way/while waiting for robin(alteratively you can parsnip go brr in the house, done by spamming left click + c on the parsnip box, which is more time efficient so gives you much more leniency).
- Run up to the mountain and stand in Robin's way as she leaves her bedroom to speed her up. Then interact with the counter as she is next to it to buy the house upgrade(2nd option on menu).
- Pass out and sleep until spring 4.

### Day 4
- Read the mail, then use the beach warp totem to get to Krobus faster.
- Give him the void ghost pendant. Teleport home and sleep for 3 days.
- Time ends on the game saving the night he moves in, which will be the autosplit for the start of day 7/end day 6, then go look at Krobus.
