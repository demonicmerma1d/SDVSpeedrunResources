# Route
## Overview
The main mechanic is to repeatedly read the mail to get different effects applied, each of which help towards specific aspects of perfection. The main limiters are Fishing, Eradication, and Friendship. Note that you never need to worry about garbage items entering the inventory, as they will all be cleared upon the next read.

## File Creation
You need to be in Chinese, select Meadowlands Farm, Legacy RNG, and Male character. Copy paste the shorter name into the player's name, and use whatever you want for farm name / favorite. Don't skip the intro cutscene, and watch it until Robin first says the player's name (at which point you skip)
## Day 1
Leave the house, moving the bed closer to the door as you do. Place down some bombs, and read your first mail.
### Read 1 (Setup)
Your inventory contains the following items
 - Tools: Irrelevant
 - Beach Warp: Immidiately Use
 - Excess Bombs: Irrelevant
 - Cookies: Irrelevant
 - Napalm Ring: Equip
 - Crab Pots: Place on the beach and the river. ToDo: Do better seed searching and create a reference image for which ones are necessary (if you do enough, you can get all the crab pot fish)
 - Magic Rock Candy and Triple Shot Espresso: Immediately Eat
Run to Marnie (through the opened community shortcut) and buy the first chicken. Note that the paste begins with a newline, so you don't need to delete the randomly generated chicken name.
### Chicken 1
This chicken starts Accelerated Kent PTSD. You can find more information on it at [the glitch info](/glitches/Y1KentFriendship.md) page on it, but the basics are:
 - If you are doing Move Breaking (consistent, requires using 3420 [79]'s), you need to press OK *before* the farmer reaches the fridge. Spam click OK until they're all gone. Then, walk up to the move trigger spot (either above the hallway, 2 below the fridge, 1 below the fridge, or the fridge, depending on how late you pressed OK).
 - If you are doing Regular (inconsistent, slightly quicker, requires using less [79]'s [I use 2268; if you click faster you might need more, if you click slower you might need less]), you need to press OK *after* the farmer reaches the fridge. Spam click OK until they're all gone. If you go past the desired dialogue box, remove [79] in units of 36, and if you end early add [79]'s in the same batch size.

In both cases, you then need to do the central loop.
 - When Kent asks the question, click the middle option. This will give +50 (*1.1 from friendship101) friendship points, and thus you just need to do this 46 times.
 - You can read the Book Of Stars while you have control; so inputs are holding right click (or X) and spamming click. Make sure to not be interacting with a furniture item (like the fridge)
 - Once you've reached 10 hearts, you can either Beach Warp out or skip cutscene (the button exists it's just invisible) and run out.

From there, head to the Wizard (and read the Book Of Stars that weren't gotten to yet). Gift the Wizard 4 stardrop teas, and then go build Obelisks + Golden Clock. Each obelisk is useful, so it's recommended to use a layout where you have easy access to all 4 from the mail. You can use Left to access Golden Clock (1 click) and Island Obelisk (2 clicks) slightly faster.

Once the Obelisks have been built, run to the Wizard's basement. There, you can find the golden rename statue. Grab Name 2, and paste that at the end of your name (no need to delete anything). Then, transition from Male to Female. If the paste was successful, a chat message will pop up complaining about the item ids. Finally, run to the pentagram and use the bombs to kill the kids. The bombs can kill after you warp out, so if you want to play risky you can warp early.

Beach warp, and run to Marnie again. Note again that the paste begins with a newline, so you don't need to delete the randomly generated chicken name.

### Chicken 2
This chicken warps you into the sewers, with a cutscene. Wait until the Dwarf dialogue box appears, and then skip. Say hi to Krobus (you can't gift until you talk once, so there's no risk with what item you hold [unless it's a warp totem. I recommend switching to slot 1 beforehand due to this]). Run out of the sewers, and go to Pierre's to get 2 backpack upgrades. Gift Pierre a Stardrop Tea (do not talk to him - he says your name). At this point, you use the Return Scepter to return home. Equip the Golden Spur in your inventory at some point. Place the Junimo Chest with the 999 UndoButtons somewhere accessible (for if anything goes wrong), and read the mail.

### Read 2 (Shipping)
This read empties your inventory and gives you items to ship. Ship them all (you can drag with left+right click, or shift+right click, to do it quickly).

### Read 8 (Crafting)
Craft most of Page 3 - you can't craft the forage seeds.

### Read 9 (Crafting)
Craft Page 4.

### Read 10 (Crafting)
Craft Page 5.

### Read 11 (Crafting)
Craft the forage seeds (on Page 3) and Page 6.

### Read 12 (Cooking)
This gives Cooking ingredients. Cook the entirety of rows 1 and 2.

### Read 13 (Cooking)
Cook rows 3, 4, and all but 1 of 5.

Cook all remaining items. Place down the Tent Kit and sleep.

### Overnight
You want double-right on fishing and double-left on combat, without caring for the other 3 professions. It goes Farming->Fishing->Foraging-Mining-Combat, so switch which side you're clicking on inbetween 2 and 5. Note that E/Escape work to close the non-profession popups, along with the shipping screen at the end of the day.

## Day 2
### BEFORE MAIL
It is imperative you eat the Magic Rock Candy before reading the mail, as the +6 luck buff it gives is factored into the Volcano manipulation.

 - - Magic Rock Candy and Triple Shot Expresso.

 - - Return Scepter

 - Row 2: Fishing + Warps.
 - - Advanced Iridium Rod, for the Jelly fishing performed on Day 3.
 - - Magic Rock Candy and Triple Shot Expresso

 - Row 3: Extra Inventory

 - - 4 stacks of different quality Sunfish, to ensure you can pick one up if you catch it while fishing.

Use the Island Obelisk to warp to the Island. Run to Leo, and skip the cutscene immidiately (sufficient to meet him as long as you open your inventory at any point in the entire rest of the run). Use the Island Warp to leave, as that is faster than running back. Run up to the Volcano, where oodles of Magma Sprites will be available for killing.

Once 150 have been killed, Mountain Warp and run to the Mines. Use the elevator to go to floor 45, and ladder down to an infested floor 49 and eradicate Dust Sprites (the first eradication message will be Grubs, so ignore that). Once Dust Sprites have been eradicated, take the elevator to floor 75, and eradicate skeletons. Return Scepter once eradicated.

### Read 17 (RNG Manip)

Read the mail, which replaces your return scepter with a tent kti. This mail read ensures the crab pots (which will process overnight) will have good results. Go to sleep.

## Day 3
### Read 18 (Cave Jelly + Void Spirits)
Read the mail, and use the Mountain Obelisk to go to the mountain. Use the elevator to go to floor 20, and catch the tutorial fish (allowing it into inventory) along with the White Algae and the Cave Jelly. If you get the Green Algae, take note.

Take the elevator down to floor 95, and loop 96 until Void Spirits are eradicated. Warp home.
### Read 19 (River Jelly + Duggies + Fish Pond)
Read the mail, and use the Mountain Obelisk. Catch a River Jelly on the way to the mines, and if you haven't caught a Green Algae yet fish for it on floor 20. Loop floor 23 until both Rock Crabs and Duggies are eradicated. Then, go to Robin and buy a Fish Pond. Warp home, and sleep.

## Day 4 -> 11
Sleep

## Day 12
### Read 20 (Fishing)

Switch to the Fishing row. Read your mail. This creates the following inventory

- Row 1: Fishing Rod, Chair, 10 fish
- Row 2: Fishing Rod, 11 fish
- Row 3: Fishing Rod, 8 fish

Perform [fish pond fishing](/glitches/FishPondFishing.md). In summary, place the chair facing the top row of the fish pond, get in, get out, and fish into the pond. Once a fish is caught, you can animation cancel when the fish is fully caught (held in the white box above your head) to delete it or allow it into your inventory and don't forget. Empty the pond, and switch to the next fish. All left over fish will get trashed by the paste, so as long as it doesn't lead to catching fish multiple times it's fine. Once a row is complete, swap to the next row.

Once every fish is caught, sleep.

## Day 13
### Read 21 (Fishing)

Read your mail, which refills the inventory with fish. Get in and out of the chair, and catch all the remaining fish.

### Read 22 (Ocean Jelly + Crab Pots + Friendship)

Read the mail again, and eat the speed buffs. Use the Ocean Obelisk to go to the ocean, and fish for the Sea Jelly and Seaweed. Then, use the dagger to make harvesting the crab pots slightly faster, and run into the festival. 

### Read 23 (Crab Pot Fish, Optional)

This mail only executes if you didn't get all 7 crab pot fish in the crab pots. It gives the remaining crab pot fish to use the [fish pond fishing](/glitches/FishPondFishing.md) glitch for.

### Read 24 (RNG Manip)

Read the mail, and then don't take a step. Your step count has been set up to get the appropriate RNG for the Calico Statues, and carefully inch so as to place a tent kit and go to sleep.

## Day 14

Tent kit to sleep, ensure no steps are taken.

## Day 15
### Read 25 (Pepper Rex, Calico Statues)
Read the mail, which sets up a combat inventory. Desert Obelisk to the desert, and run to the Skull Caverns. 

Depending on the current RNG manip, the contents of the Calico Statues are liable to change. Currently, you must touch 12 statues to get Serpent Invasion, Speed, 4 More Monsters buffs, and 6 irrelevant buffs. Floor 7 (127) is filled with Dinosaurs. Once you have both eradicated Dinos and touched 12 statues, warp home.

### Read 26 (Sandy, Slimes, Serpents, Mummies)
Read mail, desert obelisk, and speak to Sandy. Then run to the caverns, and eradicate Mummies, Serpents, and Slimes. Additionally, the next 4 statues contain 2 More-Monsters buffs, so if you wish to click them, you can.

### Read 27 (Summit)
This mail gives you 10 hearts with Sandy (who you just met), along with tent kits so you can use the Mountain Obelisk and run to Railroad. Use a tent kit just below the summit.

Because perfection only gets updated overnight (and we injected the boulder away so we can run up to the summit despite that), we sleep. Running up would lead to Mr. Qi. Killing You.
