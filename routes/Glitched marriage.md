# Route

## Notes
- Version 1.2(or earlier, just use 1.2).
- Any gender.
- Any Farm type.
- Chinese.
- This main route applies to all marriage subcatagories except Sebastian, Player marriage and Krobus.

## The Run
1) Setup the farmer name to be what is in the relevent candidates paste link, and the farm name to be what is in there as well(these are using dialogue extension so you are required to copy paste it in). Do not skip the introduction.

2) Press Esc 2x to skip through the intro cutscenes. When robin's dialogue box starts [WSC](/glitches/Window%20Shift%20Cancel.md) to allow all the dialogue to process, close the menu and press F to skip the cutscene. If you are too fast you will not get all the items however on this scale it is unlikely.

3) Blow yourself up with the 5 megabombs. Optimally(which only matters for Harvey marriage) this is playing a bomb every 1s, since a bomb has a fuse of 2s, and the player gains 1s of iframes when getting hit. You should die, you can low roll on damage sometimes however which bricks the run.

4) WSC through all textboxes in the death cutscene, until you can skip the cutscene, then skip it. You should have a mermaid pendant at this point in slot 6. This cutscene met the npc, and gave you 10 hearts of friendship.

5) If this is Harvey marriage, you are placed by his bedroom door, go into his bedroom, propose to him, WSC the dialogue, and immeadietly teleport home with the return scepter. If it is not Harvey, go to wherever the NPC lives and wait for them to be accessible, either through them leaving their house early, or by going inside the house when possible to propose to them. Exact details are below.

6) Sleep 3 days, [save skipping](/glitches/Miscellaneous%20Glitches.md) each time, then WSC through the dialogue in the marriage cutscene. Note that even tho you WSC, the dialogue is invisibly there, but instantly loaded so you have to spam click through to clear it to progress. Time ends on the kiss (marriage autosplit).

## Notes on NPC differences
If the NPC is not mentioned here, the default is following the blueprint above. By "sleep" by default assume you should be saveskipping unless otherwise stated. 
- Sam : Sleep until Spring 6th before blowing yourself up (he goes outside at 8am if its sunny).
- Maru : Sleep until spring 2nd before blowing yourself up (she leaves her house at 8am).
