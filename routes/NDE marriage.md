# Route
### Important Notes
- Run on version 1.6
- Chinese
- Exact details of which days you are active, and which farm paste to use depend on the NPC.

## The General Run(Shane)
Setup the file with the pastes. The route detailed below is for Shane(fastest),for other candidates consult below the Shane route.

### Intro
Watch the intro(press Esc 2x) until you receive items from the player name in the busstop, then skip the cutscene.

### First Active Day(Spring 1)
1) Eat a magic rock candy(MRC).
2) Exhaust to low energy (parsnip go brr) on the parsnip box(spam alternate left click and c by default on keyboard, c is the bind for leftclick)
3) Gift Shane most likely when he leaves Marnie's, then go into town.
4) At 9am, enter pierre's, talk to Pierre, sell 3 MRC, gift Abigail a MRC.
5) Use the mountain warp totem, go to Robin's, buy 450 wood, upgrade the house and pass out.

### Second Active Day(Spring 4)
Note: House should be upgraded between the first and second day.
1) Near exhaust on the parsnip box(you will not have time to exhaust otherwise for faster candidates), wait outside Marnie's door for Shane to leave, preexhaust using a tool to -14 energy.
2) Gift Shane a MRC.
3) Gift Shane 8 stardrop tea, the bouquet, 2 more stardrop tea, then the mermaid pendant.
4) Passout and sleep until the wedding, time on kiss(there is a marriage autosplit).

## Maru
- On the second active day(Spring 4), run up to Robin's and preexhaust outside there instead of going down to Marnie's, giving the MRC, stardrop tea and other items to Maru instead of Shane.

## Abigail
- On the first active day(Spring 1), gift Abigail all 8 stardrop tea(at least 2 for room access) before using the mountain warp totem.
- On the second active day(Spring 4), preexhaust then wait for pierre's to open. Gift Shane a MRC while you wait(final 2 teas). At 9am run into Abigail's room, finish gifting her the bouquet, the teas, and the pendant.

## Emily
- Use the alterative beach warp farm name(this is the one with `[690]` in instead of `[689]`)
- Sleep to Spring 2 for the first active day
- Gift Maru as she comes into town before going into Pierre's as normal. Walk to Robin's to upgrade your house as she walks past the counter(click the counter when shes near). 
- Warp to the beach, go to emily, gift emily at least 2 stardrop tea before passing out.
- Second active day moves to Spring 5, preexhaust outside while waiting for 9am, and give her the rest of the items, inc. mermaid pendant. I recommend just gifting everything except the pendant on the first active day for convinience.

## Haley
- Use the alterative beach warp totem farm name.
- On the first active day(Spring 1), walk to Robin for house upgrade and teleport to beach to access Haley as she leaves her room at 10am, and gift her the 8 stardrop tea(at least 2 for room access) before passing out.
- On the Second active day preexhaust, gift shane a MRC and finish giving the items, similar to Abigail.

## Leah
- Do not use any warp totems, this is more timeloss than walking everywhere. 
- Leah's access time on the first active day(Spring 1) is 11am and you can just walk back from Robin's in time. 
- Gift her 2 stardrop tea for room access, if you give more you will have to watch the unskippable cutscene on the second active day.
- On the second active day, gift shane a MRC for the remaining stardrop tea and preexhaust outside leah while waiting for 10am to go inside(it is better to slow walk to her, especially if you place a chair and chair hop inside)

## Penny
- On second active day(Spring 4), gift Shane a MRC for the extra stardrop tea and be preexhausted outside Penny and Pam's trailer for her to leave at 8am. Gift her.

## Elliott
- Sleep to Spring 5 for the first active day.
- Do not use warp totems this is more timeloss than walking everywhere.
- Gift him 8 stardrop tea(at least 2 for room access) on the first active day.
- Second active day moves to Spring 8, gift shane an MRC for remaining tea, preexhaust outside his cabin and finish giving Elliott the items when you can enter at 10am. 

## Sebastian
- Sleep to Spring 2 for the first active day.
- Gift Maru **and** Shane a MRC while waiting for Pierre's to open at 9am
- Walk up to Robin's for the house upgrade(not warp) and use her as she passes the counter.
- Second active day on Spring 6(weather:sunny), exahust to near 0 energy and wait outside Robin's for 9am. Exhaust to -14(7 swings from 0) outside Sebastian's door while he starts leaving his room at 9am. Gift Sebastian the items in order.
- If Spring 6 is raining, there is a 50% chance he leaves his room at 10:30am, and a 50% chance he leaves at 3pm, there is not anything you can do but wait/reset particularly.

## Alex
- On the second active day(Spring 4) gift shane a MRC for the remaining tea, at the bottom left entrance to town to minimise how much time running back to Alex's house(unlocks at 8am). 

## Sam
- Sleep to Spring 2 for the first active day.
- Gift Shane **and** Maru a MRC while waiting for Pierre to open at 9am.
- Second active day on Spring 6(weather:sunny). If it is raining this is bad RNG, either Sam is accessible at 11am(50%) or 3pm(50%). 
- Exhaust to near 0 energy outside Sam's house while waiting for 9am, give him the items.

## Harvey
- Sleep to Spring 2 for the first active day.
- Gift Shane **and** Maru a MRC while waiting for Pierre to open at 9am.
- Second active day on Spring 6, preexhaust to low energy outside the clinic.
- If it is sunny, Harvey leaves at 8:30am and you can exhaust to -14 outside and gift him everything outside.
- If it is raining, exhaust to near 0 energy and run inside at 9am to gift him the items.
