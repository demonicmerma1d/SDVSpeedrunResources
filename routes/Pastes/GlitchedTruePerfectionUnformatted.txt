Legacy RNG, Meadowlands, Boy.
Watch intro cutscene until Robin gives beach warp, and skip.
Read mail, eat buffs, bomb front yard. Once sufficient empty space for obelisks + fish pond, beach warp.
Buy chicken (no need to delete characters; we have newlines at start). While performing Accelerated Kent PTSD, read ~40 of your books. Run to the wizard while reading the other 20.
At the wizard, give the wizard 4 stardrop teas, build the obelisks (accessible) and golden clock. Run to his basement. At statue, transition (disabling name 1) + paste name 2 at the end of name 1. Take warp to Witch Hut, and use Infinity Dagger to kill 200 bats.
Beach warp, and buy chicken 2. The moment the dwarf says something, skip cutscene. Say hi to Krobus, and then leave the sewers. 
Run to Pierre and buy backpack upgrades. Give pierre a gift (don't talk).
Island Warp, run to Leo. skip cutscene immidiately (counts as meeting),
Mountain Warp, run to Robin. Build Fish Pond. (If you're fast, you might need to wait. probably could route in one of the later mails before if necessary)
Return Scepter, complete shipping + crafting + cooking.
Mail2-5 are just shipping, sell it all
Mail6 is shipping. Place down cookout kit
Mail7 place workbench, craft page 1 & 2
Mail8 craft page 3 except for wild seeds
Mail9 craft page 4 
Mail10 craft page 5
Mail11 craft wild seeds & page 6
Mail12-14 cook everything you can (it's in sequence, avoid cooking miners treat twice)
Mail15 place down miniforge and cook the rest.

Mail16 forge perfect weapons; triple ruby hammer, triple aqua dagger, both weapons artful, and make both rings napalm+iridium band.
Put explosive ammo in slingshot, and deluxe bait + dressed spinners in advanced iridium rod.

Sleep. (Spring 1 -> Spring 2)

Read mail, and get resources to enchant weapons + create a napalm+iridium band ring. Goal enchantments are both Artful (+75 crit power is 1/14 and game1.random that's probably too icky to roll for).
Optional: Abandon the hammer you basically never use it and it does cost like 10 seconds to craft.
Obelisk to Volcano. 
Once reached Volcano 9 and killed sprites, mountain warp.
Loop Floor 49 until Dust Sprites are eradicated [also grubs] - if it's not a dust sprite layout, warp home, read mail, mountain warp, go back to 49.

Once Dust Sprites are eradicated, go to floor 70 and eradicate skeletons there.

Sleep. (Spring 2 -> Spring 3)

Volcano till floor 9, mountain warp.
Go to floor 20, and catch the tutorial fish (likely sunfish, could be stonefish), and let it into your inventory (there will be inventory space). Then, catch a cave jelly (guaranteed good luck) followed by white algae. Staircase down to 23 to kill duggies.
Once done, warp home, read mail, and FISH A RIVER JELLY out of the mountain lake, along with green algae if it hadn't been caught yet. Then, go to 96 and loop until rock crabs eradicated [also void spirits].

Sleep. (Spring 3 -> Spring 4)

Volcano till floor 9 or sprites eradicated. Desert warp.
Go to floor 7, and eradicate dinos (same reroll strat - if it's a mummy floor / a layout that can't get infested, you shouldn't warp home). Any good mummy/other floor can be bombed too. Then, warp home and read, and eradicate Royal Serpents [and slimes {and mummies}] on floor 1.
MEET SANDY MEET SANDY

Sleep. (Spring 4 -> Spring 5)
Optional bonus Volcano day if sprites aren't eradicated yet. (I think I miscounted when first routing, I think it's actually 4/5 volcano days not 3/4. so you're doing today + maybe tomorrow too)

Sleep a bunch! (Spring 5 -> Spring 12)
Read mail (have advanced iridium rod as primary row), and inventory transitions to be perfectly fishy. Fish pond glitch the full inventory (place the chair facing the top tile of fish pond [can be sideways, top, or bottom] and hop in + out to get good positioning)

Sleep. (Spring 12 -> 13)
Read mail, and get a second inventory of fishes. Fish pond glitch this full inventory.
Read your mail, and beach warp to catch sea jelly (guaranteed good rng) and then seaweed (it sucks). Run up to the egg festival, and say hi to every person (not to Robin, not to Lewis, not to Pierre, since you've met them already).
Leave, and read mail. Mountain warp, and place a tent kit in front of the summit. 

Sleep (Spring 13 -> 14)

Walk onto the summit!!!!

//The Great Rng Manip Section
/*
The basic strategy is as follows:
We can detect if we are creating a random with a negative number or a positive number as a seed by seeing how it changes when we add to it
We can create a negative seed by using a string with a known large negative hash (in this case b\nOOL for the comedic value; hashes to -1073742666)
By adding (Seed + -1 billion + X), and then checking if it's positive or negative, we can perform binary search on an X value that makes it zero
Specifically, we notice a success of a 48% chance and then add 11; this moves the rng ranges by about 0.25 in possible directions.
Once we've binary searched to nearby, we transition to linear search.
We end up with DaysPlayed such that Seed + -1billion + X = 0.

Now, to control a CreateDaySaveRandom to have a value Y, you would do the following:
	a) Divide DaysPlayed by 2; now seed/2 + -1073742666/2 + X/2 = 0 i.e. seed/2 + X/2 = 1073742666/2
	b) Add Y - 1073742666/2 to DaysPlayed.
To control the Volcano floors, which have DaysPlayed * (floor + 1), divide by floor+1 and use the modulo to detect which of multiple layouts you achieve.

In this run, the following specific controls are performed:
	a) On the first read of Spring 2nd, we force-load volcano floors to have lots of magma sprites. This requires eating the iridium magic rock candy.
	b) After that, we leave the rng so that Floor 49 is infested *and* that there will be the correct items in the crab pots
	c) On the first read of Spring 3rd, we make floor 23 infested and ensure second fish caught will have good jelly rng. (These are done purely by offset? Maybe? Check optimality)
	d) On the second read of Spring 3rd, we make the first fish caught have good jelly rng
	e) On the second read of Spring 13th, we make the first fish caught have good jelly rng
	f) On the third read of Spring 13th, we set stepsTaken so that the RNG on the 15th will be as desired. 
	g) On the first read of Spring 15th, we make floor ?? a dino floor and ensure the first ?? calico statues are good
	h) On the second read of Spring 15th, we make floor 1 infested and ensure the next ?? calico statues are good
*/
